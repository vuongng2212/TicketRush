## 🎯 KEY DESIGN INNOVATIONS

### 1. Kinetic Scramble Hero ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Headline text scrambles chaotically on load
- Settles with spring physics animation
- Repeats every 8 seconds
- **Impact:** Grabs attention, premium feel, memorable

### 2. Electric Glass Cards ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Glassmorphic design (blur: 14px)
- Gradient cyan + magenta borders
- Neon glow on hover
- **Impact:** Premium, tactile, exciting interaction

### 3. Neon Pulse Buttons ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Lime-to-cyan gradient
- Shine animation (3s loop)
- Glow intensifies on hover
- **Impact:** CTAs impossible to miss

### 4. Morphing Orbs Background ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- 3-4 animated circles with gradients
- Float infinitely (staggered timing)
- Creates depth without distraction
- **Impact:** Professional, energetic vibe

### 5. Glassmorphic Navbar ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Semi-transparent dark background
- Blur effect + electric border
- Sticky on scroll
- **Impact:** Modern, premium, fintech-aligned
