## 🎨 ELECTRIC PULSE DESIGN SYSTEM

### Color Palette
```
Primary Brand Colors:
  🔵 Electric Blue    #00d4ff   (neon cyan - main accent)
  🔴 Hot Magenta      #ff2d7b   (hot pink - urgency)
  🟢 Lime Rush        #39ff14   (neon lime - success)
  🟡 Acid Yellow      #eaff00   (bright yellow - highlights)
  🟣 Electric Purple  #c91dff   (neon purple - secondary)

Background Colors:
  ⬛ Dark BG          #0a0a0a   (primary background)
  ⬜ Surface          #1a1a1a   (cards, surfaces)
  ⬜ Surface Light    #252d4a   (hover states)

Text Colors:
  ⚪ Primary          #ffffff   (main text)
  🔘 Secondary        #b0b5c1   (supporting text)
  ⚫ Tertiary         #7a7f94   (disabled text)
```

### Typography System
- **Display Font:** Space Grotesk (headlines, kinetic text)
- **Body Font:** Inter (body text, UI)
- **Mono Font:** Inter Mono (metadata, prices, code)
- **Sizes:** H1→Caption (64px down to 12px)

### Animation System
- **Kinetic Spring:** stiffness 300, damping 20 (snappy, energetic)
- **Smooth Spring:** stiffness 200, damping 25 (smooth, polished)
- **Bouncy Spring:** stiffness 400, damping 15 (playful, bouncy)

### Spacing System
- **xs:** 4px | **sm:** 8px | **md:** 12px | **lg:** 16px
- **xl:** 24px | **xxl:** 32px | **xxxl:** 48px
